Juice Cleanse

Rest the stomach, rest and repair the gut, rest the liver, reduce your appetite, ease food decision-making, eliminate harmful foods, lose weight, improve energy, heal our cells those are some of the benefits of juice cleanse. Our body have a natural healing power, with Pure Bar juice cleansing you will unlock your body's healing and do your first step into a healthy lifestyle. You will never believe how something that tasty can be that healthy!


Before The Cleanse

DIET

A light diet 3 days before, consisting of only organic products such as fruits,vegetables,eggs,whole grains and 8 glasses of water a day will definitely make you ready to do the cleanse.

Avoid: alcohol,caffeine,sugar,processed foods.


TIME

For the best experience of juice cleanse, make free time. If you have an emotionally intense lifestyle, it will change your life and the way you think about food, and, most important you will enjoy it more.

Tell your friends and family about the benefits and they will join you or support you.


ATTITUDE

Everything is temporary, juice cleanse is just the beginning of what you can do for body. Even if you feel hunger and want to eat, there is a purpose to this process.

Stay with it and you will get the result you are expecting.


During The Cleanse

DIET

We recommend, to concentrate on the juice cleanse, 100% juicing.

Eating slows down the process. If you need to eat, choose only organic. Fruits, vegetables, soaked nuts and seeds. Kale chips can work as well.


EXERCISE

Limit yourself to light workouts such as walks, yoga and pilates.

Your energy level will be different. See what your body tells you.


ACTIVITIES

While you are juicing take it one level up. Don't just treat your body, treat your mind also. Avoid places with loud music, go for a walk in the park, meditate, relax etc.

Your body is your machine, how you take care of it will show.


After The Cleanse

DIET

Return slowly to eating solid foods.

You can follow the pre-cleanse diet. Drink tea, eat organic fruits and vegetables. Always be aware of what you eat.


ACTIVITIES

Don't just jump back into your normal intense lifestyle. Take your time relax.
